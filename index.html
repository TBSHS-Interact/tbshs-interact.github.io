TBSHS Interact

Who are we?

Interact is a global organisation, founded by the Rotary Club, with the aim of raising money and awareness for various charities. We aim to do our best to help our community, and the planet as a whole, in any way we can.
There are over 20 000 Interact clubs accross the globe, operating in 159 different countries, with over 450 000 members.
Interact has been running at TBSHS for many years now, and since we started, we have raised many thousands of pounds, each year, for a wide variety of charities and good causes.

How do we raise money?

We raise money within the school in a variety of ways. These range from non-uniform days, to our annual talent show - SHOWCASE.
We are particularly proud of our efforts in raising money for national campaigns, including, but not limited to, The Poppy Appeal, Comic/Sports Relief and Children in Need.

How can you get involved?

If you want to play a part in helping other people, and you are a student at The Bishop's Stortford High School, come along to Room 8 every Wednesday lunch time to join our meetings.
We will discuss new ideas for how we can continue to help our community both within, and outside of the school.

If you are not a student at TBSHS, you can still get involved with Rotary and Interact.
Find out if your own school has an Interact club, if it doesn't, create one.
Go to www.rotary.org to find out more.
